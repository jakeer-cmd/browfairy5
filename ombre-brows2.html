Ombré Powder Brows

Ombré Powder Brows: The perfect solution for those who love a soft, powdered makeup look that lasts. This advanced technique creates a beautiful gradient effect—lighter at the front of the brow and gradually darker toward the tail.

Using a digital micropigmentation machine, we implant pigment in a shading pattern that mimics the look of makeup powder. Unlike microblading, this technique doesn't create individual hair strokes but instead provides a soft, filled appearance that's perfect for all skin types, including oily or mature skin.

Why Choose Browfairy PMU for Ombré Powder Brows?

  • Expert artistry by Ridhima – a certified permanent makeup specialist with extensive experience in ombré brow techniques.
  • Customized brow design that complements your facial features and personal style.
  • Premium-quality pigments that heal to a beautiful, natural shade and last for years.
  • Advanced techniques that ensure minimal discomfort and excellent healing results.

Ombré Powder Brows are ideal for clients who want a more defined, makeup-like look without the daily hassle of filling in their brows. They're also an excellent choice for those with oily skin, sparse brows, or previous tattoo work that needs correction.
