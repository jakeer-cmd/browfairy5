Lash lift and tint

Lash Lift & Tint is a low-maintenance beauty treatment that enhances your natural lashes by giving them a lifted, curled, and darker appearance — without the need for extensions or daily mascara. The lash lift gently curls the lashes from the root, while the tint deepens their color for a more defined, eye-opening effect.

This treatment is perfect for anyone wanting naturally longer-looking, fuller lashes with minimal effort.

The result is beautifully lifted and tinted lashes that make your eyes appear brighter, more awake, and effortlessly enhanced — lasting up to 6–8 weeks.
