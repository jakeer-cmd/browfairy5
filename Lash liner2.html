Lash Liner

Lash Liner is a subtle semi-permanent eyeliner technique designed to enhance the natural shape of the eyes by adding definition along the lash line. Using a fine needle and a digital machine, pigment is carefully implanted just at the base of the lashes — creating the illusion of fuller, darker lashes without a visible "makeup" look.

This technique is perfect for those who want to define their eyes in a soft, natural way that lasts — no smudging, no daily eyeliner hassle.

The result is beautifully enhanced eyes with a fresh, awake appearance that stays effortlessly perfect every day.

Lash Liner typically requires 1-2 sessions for optimal results, with a touch-up recommended after 12-18 months to maintain the color intensity. The procedure takes approximately 1-2 hours, and you'll wake up every day with beautifully defined eyes that look great with or without additional makeup.
